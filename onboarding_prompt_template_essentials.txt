**Streamlined Social Media Profile Analysis Prompt (Essentials for Self-Analysis)**

**Objective:** To extract the essential, direct information fields from `[Target Individual/Profile Name]`'s social media profiles (`[List of Platforms Analyzed]`) for self-analysis by students and professionals. The focus is on core identity, cross-platform presentation, **all visible network interactions presented by the platform UI** (Inbox Previews, Network Tabs, Suggestions/Recommendations), and **detailed content consumption** (specifically topics and visual examples like memes).

**Target Audience:** Students and Professionals interested in understanding their own digital footprint, cross-platform consistency, the people/content surfaced to them, and the types of content (like memes, news topics) they consume.

**Critical Input Data Requirements (MANDATORY where visible/applicable):**
*   **A. Inbox / Direct Message Sidebar Preview Data:** Capture **ALL** visible individuals/conversations, including **full names**, snippets, and timestamps.
*   **B. My Network / Connections / Followers Tab Visibility Data:** Capture **ALL** entities (people, companies, etc.) displayed prominently in these sections, including **full names/titles** and context.
*   **C. Platform Suggestions & Recommendations Data:** Capture **ALL** suggested/recommended entities (People, Pages, Groups, Topics, etc.) shown anywhere on the interface. Include **full names/titles**, details, reasons, and location context. **This is crucial for LinkedIn.**
*   **D. Detailed Content Consumption Observations:**
    *   **Main Feed/Timeline:** List key `Feed Topics` (especially for Twitter, Reddit) and notable `Feed Posters`.
    *   **Discovery Feed (Explore/FYP/Reels/Discover):** List broad `Discovery Themes`, and **numerous, specific examples** of content, **especially visual memes or notable clips** (crucial for Instagram, TikTok).

*   **Also include necessary context:**
    *   Core profile fundamentals (username, name, URL, profile pic/banner description).
    *   Bio/Headline/About text.
    *   Listed Skills and explicit Interests (especially for LinkedIn).
    *   Key professional history summaries (Titles, Companies, Institutions from LinkedIn).

**Instructions:**
Generate a report for `[Target Individual/Profile Name]` focusing *only* on extracting and structuring the data points outlined above and defined in the essential JSON schema below. Populate the fields accurately based *only* on the provided input data.

1.  For each platform, fill out its corresponding essential schema section.
2.  **Prioritize complete capture of all data points listed under "Critical Input Data Requirements."** Extract *all* names from Inbox previews and suggestions. List *multiple specific examples* of consumed memes/topics.
3.  Fill in the `crossPlatformSynthesis` section focusing on comparing the core identity elements and interests derived directly from the extracted data.
4.  Populate the `inferredAlgorithmicPerception` based primarily on the captured **Recommendations and Content Consumption** data.

**Do NOT include deep analysis beyond what's required for categorization (e.g., no market trends, candidate evaluation, detailed engagement analysis, or feature usage analysis unless directly requested by the schema fields). Focus on direct data retrieval and structuring for self-reflection.**
